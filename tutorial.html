Welcome to the tutorial!

Maybe you're here because you don't know how to play my Dogecoin Clicker. Maybe not. But here is how to play.

When you first start the game, it will ask you how much dogecoin you need. If you guess right, you get an reward!
Once you have guessed, you click on the coin and gain dogecoin. Once you have enough, you can by Auto Click.
Once you have auto click, you can get more dogecoin! Keep gaining dogecoin until you have 5,000. Then sell it to get doge gems!
You should then be on the moon. Don't stop there, you can get to Mars!
